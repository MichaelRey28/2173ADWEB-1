About

We believe that in transpareny, cooperation globally, and the free exchange of talent and ideas. A forum for the sharing of these techincal and humanitarian discussions is given for meetings, conferences and other events. We are also committed to creating a secure, constructive and supportive atmosphere for all participants in conferences, activities, and meetings who participate in these discussions with us.


Conferences are a part of the legacy of this organization and continue today to be a catalyst for creativity. We are committed to developing strategies that support and expand the conference activities throughtout the world
